# MnemoSphere — User Guide

## Getting Started

### 1. Create an Account
1. Open MnemoSphere in your browser
2. Click "Register" on the home page
3. Enter your name, email, and password
4. Click "Register" — you'll be logged in automatically

### 2. Add Your First Note
1. Navigate to **Notes** page
2. Enter a **Title** (e.g., "Python Basics")
3. Type or paste your **Content** in the text area
4. Add **Tags** (e.g., "python, programming")
5. Click **Save Note**
6. The AI will automatically generate a summary

### 3. Review Notes (1-3-7 Method)
1. Navigate to **Review** page
2. See all notes due for review
3. Click a note to start the review session
4. Answer AI-generated multiple-choice questions
5. After answering, see explanation and feedback
6. Complete all questions for the note
7. Score ≥70% advances to next review stage

### 4. Track Your Progress
1. Navigate to **Dashboard** for an overview
2. See total notes, reviews completed, and memory score
3. Check which notes are due for review
4. Navigate to **Analytics** for detailed stats

### 5. Search Your Knowledge
1. On the **Notes** page, type a question in the search bar
2. Click **Search**
3. MnemoSphere finds relevant notes using AI
4. Click **Clear** to return to all notes

## Tips for Best Results

- **Add notes regularly:** The more content you add, the better the AI adapts
- **Review daily:** Check the Review page each day for due items
- **Use tags:** Tag notes by topic for better analytics
- **Be detailed:** More content = better summaries and quizzes
- **Score matters:** Answer honestly to help the AI adapt difficulty

## FAQ

**Q: What happens when I complete all 3 review stages?**
A: The note is marked as "mastered" and won't appear in review unless you manually delete and re-add it.

**Q: Can I upload PDFs or images?**
A: Currently text input is supported. Upload support is coming soon.

**Q: How does the AI generate questions?**
A: The AI analyzes your note content and generates personalized questions using the Groq LLM.

**Q: Is my data private?**
A: Yes. Your notes are stored in your personal account. Only you can access them.
